starts
 
in
 
Texas
 
Hold’Em
 
mode.
 
There
 
are
 
two
 
ways
 
to
 
switch
 
between
 
game
 
variants:
 
1.
 
Touch
 
the
 
‘HOLDEM’
 
button
 
in
 
Action
 
Tracker,
 
or
  
2.
 
Follow
 
the
 
‘Tag
 
Player’
 
procedure
 
to
 
create
 
special
 
Player
 
ID
 
cards
 
with
 
specific
 
names
 
(see
 
table
 
below).
