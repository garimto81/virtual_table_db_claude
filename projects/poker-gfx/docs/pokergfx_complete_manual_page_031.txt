playback

social

●

the
 
game
 
as

in
 
the
 
previous
 
section;
  
●

Tracker

(see

chapter

Action

how

/g0

‘TAG’

to
 
do

●
 
To

the
 
special
 
name
 
tag

the
 
muck
 
antenna

●
 
When

‘Split
